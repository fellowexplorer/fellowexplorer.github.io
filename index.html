About

I'm an AI engineer and builder passionate about creating tools that push the boundaries of what's possible with artificial intelligence. My work spans from generative AI and large language models to multi-agent systems and production ML infrastructure.

I believe the best way to understand AI is to build with it. That's why I spend my time creating open-source projects, writing about practical AI engineering, and exploring new paradigms in machine intelligence.

When I'm not coding, you'll find me reading research papers, contributing to the open-source community, or exploring new ideas at the intersection of technology and creativity.
